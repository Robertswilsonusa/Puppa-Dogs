What Is Acepromazine?

Acepromazine is a tranquilizer that helps reduce anxiety and provides mild to moderate sedation in dogs. Veterinarians often prescribe it before travel, grooming appointments, diagnostic procedures, or surgeries. It works by calming the nervous system, helping dogs remain relaxed in situations that may otherwise cause stress or fear.

Since every dog responds differently, veterinarians carefully determine the appropriate dose before prescribing the medication.
